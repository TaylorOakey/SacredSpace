07 / Sacred Artifact Hunt

The World as Sacred Zone

Not stickers. Portals. Every QR code placed in the physical world is an entry point into the mythology. The city becomes a playable map. Every park is a zone. Every zone is a chapter.

The Discovery Loop
🔍
Find
Player spots a Sacred Artifact — sticker sigil, QR tag, NFC token, or physical hidden cache. Curiosity activates.
📱
Scan
QR opens an artifact page. Card revealed. Sound activates. Quest displayed. The mythology unlocks in the moment of discovery.
⚔️
Quest
Each artifact gives one real-world action. Not abstract — concrete. The quest is calibrated to the realm of the card found.
✦
Log + XP
Player logs the find. Earns XP. Completes quest. Optional share to social. The action becomes memory in the system.
🌲
Level + Return
Progress tracked. Ranks earned. New zones unlock. The player is drawn back into the city with new purpose.
Artifact Tier System
Common Artifact
70% · Tier 1
QR sticker with realm sigil. Placed on public boards, poles, community spaces. Unlocks a card, sound loop, and a simple quest. Entry point for new seekers.
Rare Artifact
25% · Tier 2
Physical token hidden near the QR. Finding the token requires solving a short location clue. Higher XP reward. Character drop — Zii, Mylo, Auralon, or Koru.
Legendary Emergence
5% · Tier 3
Appears for 24–72 hours. Custom engraved coin, wood token, or carved sigil. Unlocks lore fragments not available anywhere else. The rarest discovery in any zone. Maximum XP.
