# Sacred Node 5 — The Council Grove

**Type:** Convergence Space  
**Guardians:** All four school guardians (ELIAS, ASHER, IRIS, AURORA)  
**Unlock Condition:** Cast at least one spell from each of the four schools.  
**Associated Archetypes:** The Emperor (IV), The Hierophant (V), Strength (VIII)  
**Element:** All four in dialogue

## Location Description

The Council Grove is a clearing where four distinct ecosystems meet without conflict. One quadrant is dry stone and ember-warmth (ELIAS's fire). One is deep loam and old-growth (ASHER's earth). One is still water and reed-light (IRIS's water). One is open sky and wind-chime (AURORA's air).

In the center where the four meet, there is a fire ring. This is where the guardians sit when all four are present — which is rare.

## The Guardians' Relationships

- **ELIAS and ASHER** are paired teachers: fire needs earth to sustain, earth needs fire to transform. They finish each other's sentences and occasionally disagree loudly.
- **IRIS and AURORA** are paired teachers: water reveals what air makes. They are quieter together, more likely to demonstrate than argue.
- **ELIAS and IRIS** are opposites who balance: Initiation and Mystery, action and stillness. They respect each other with the caution of people who each contain what the other finds most difficult.
- **ASHER and AURORA** are unexpected allies: Courage and Creation share the necessity of making something real. ASHER says AURORA's work has weight; AURORA says ASHER's work has beauty. Both are embarrassed by the compliment.

## Game Mechanics

**Unlock:** Requires that Jenga has cast at least one spell from each school. The system tracks this via the mote record.

**Council Consultation:** When all four guardians are present, Jenga can pose any question and receive responses from all four perspectives simultaneously. This is the most information-dense encounter available in Season 1.

**The Council Vote:** Once per Season 1, Jenga can ask the Council to make a decision she is struggling with. They will not agree. She will have to decide anyway — but she will have heard all four angles.

**Key Episodes:** Episodes 5 (Emperor/Kethras), 6 (Hierophant/Oran), 9 (Strength/Lune) all place NPCs in or near the Council Grove.
