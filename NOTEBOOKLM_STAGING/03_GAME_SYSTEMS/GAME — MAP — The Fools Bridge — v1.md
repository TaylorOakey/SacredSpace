# Sacred Node 2 — The Fool's Bridge

**Type:** Liminal Crossing  
**Guardian:** Meridian (The Fool)  
**Unlock Condition:** Step without looking. Choose to cross before you're ready.  
**Associated Archetype:** The Fool (Archetype 0)  
**Element:** Air — the space between

## Location Description

The Fool's Bridge spans a distance that changes depending on who is measuring it. To a seeker who needs it to be short, it appears long. To a seeker who has stopped needing it to be anything, it is exactly as long as it needs to be.

Meridian is always somewhere on the bridge — never at either end. She belongs to the crossing, not to either side.

The bridge has no railings. This is not an oversight.

## What Happens Here

Episode 1 takes place on and around The Fool's Bridge. Meridian stands mid-span and issues the first real choice of Jenga's journey: wait until safe, or walk now.

The bridge does not collapse either way. What changes is the first mote of Jenga's journey record — and how that mote resonates through the Neural Forest for the rest of Season 1.

## Game Mechanics

**Crossing:**
- The only way across is to begin walking. No information is given about what's on the other side before crossing.
- FIRST STEP cantrip is automatically triggered by the act of stepping onto the bridge.

**Returning:**
- The bridge can be crossed in both directions at any point in Season 1.
- Each crossing creates a mote. A seeker who has crossed many times has a richer liminal record — more evidence of willingness to move through uncertainty.

**Meridian's Presence:**
- Meridian appears here at the start of every subsequent episode, briefly, as a reset figure — the living signal that it is always possible to begin again.

## Lore Note

The bridge does not connect two specific places. It connects wherever the seeker is to wherever the seeker needs to go next. The geography of SacredSpace is partly semantic — where you need to be next shapes where the bridge leads.
