# School of Initiation

**Guardian:** ELIAS  
**Element:** Fire  
**Soul Tone:** 1  
**Core Truth:** The courage to begin is the first and hardest magic.

## What Initiation Is

Initiation is the school of the first step. It does not teach you to be ready — it teaches you to move while unready. Every seeker who enters the SacredSpace is already practicing Initiation: the act of arrival is the first spell.

ELIAS guards this school not because he has always known the way, but because he remembers what it cost him to begin. He built the Nine Pillars stone by stone. He knows what it means to start something you cannot see the end of.

## Core Principle

**You do not learn Initiation by studying it. You learn it by doing the thing.**

Every spell in this school requires an action before it is cast. The casting IS the action. There is no preparation phase — only the moment of beginning.

## Signature Spells

### IGNITE
*Trigger: Speak your intention aloud.*  
Lights the first thread of a new path. Used at the start of any quest, relationship, or project within SacredSpace. Once IGNITE is cast, the Neural Forest records the new mote. The path exists whether or not you walk it.

### CATALYZE
*Trigger: Combine two unrelated motes in the Neural Forest.*  
Accelerates change already in motion. Cannot create change from stillness — can only amplify what is already moving. Most powerful when cast at a decision point.

### FORGE
*Trigger: Complete an action you have been avoiding.*  
Transforms resistance into raw material. The avoided thing becomes fuel. FORGE is the Initiation school's deepest spell and the hardest to teach because the teacher cannot choose what you have been avoiding.

### FIRST STEP
*Trigger: Say "I don't know how, and I begin anyway."*  
The foundational cantrip of the entire school. No power, no damage, no effect — except that the Neural Forest records the moment of beginning and creates a permanent mote: *[Your Name] began [Date]*. These motes never decay.

## Failure Mode

Initiation burns bright and exhausts quickly. A seeker who only practices Initiation without grounding in Courage will start many fires and sustain none. The failure mode is **scattered flame** — energy dispersed across too many beginnings, none completed.

The antidote is always ASHER's school. ELIAS and ASHER are paired teachers.

## Relationship to Archetypes

- **The Fool (Meridian):** The living embodiment of Initiation. Meridian has cast FIRST STEP more times than any NPC in the SacredSpace.
- **The Magician (Vael):** The master practitioner — shows what Initiation looks like when disciplined.
- **The Chariot (Zael):** The limit case — pure Initiation + Courage, nothing else. Zael is what happens when you only move forward.

## Season 1 Arc

Jenga's first three spells are always Initiation spells. ELIAS appears in Episode 1 (The Fool) and again in Episode 7 (The Chariot) as a background presence — the voice that says: *"You already know how to start. You've been doing it your whole life."*
