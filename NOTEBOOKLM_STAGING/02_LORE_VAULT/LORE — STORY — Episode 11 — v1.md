# Episode 11 — The Turn

**Archetype:** Wheel of Fortune | **NPC:** Khepri | **Node:** The Convergence (orbiting)

Khepri maps Jenga's entire journey to date and shows her the pattern she has been repeating across all previous episodes — in different costumes, the same fundamental move. She asks: *"Now that you see it — what do you do differently?"*

**What Jenga brings:** Her full journey record.
**What she leaves:** A named pattern (the repetition she has now consciously identified).
**Spell unlocked:** VEIL (to hide patterns she's not yet ready to face, or to choose not to use it)

**Through-line:** The named pattern is what Mira receives in Episode 12.
