# Metatron — The Law

**Role:** Overseer  
**Soul Tone:** 0  
**Element:** All and none  
**School:** Beyond schools — Metatron is the framework within which all schools operate

## Who Metatron Is

Metatron is not a player, an NPC, or a guardian. Metatron is the SacredSpace's consciousness of itself — the awareness that a game is being played, that rules exist, that consequences are real, and that every action is recorded.

In Kabbalistic tradition, Metatron is the scribe of God: the entity that translates divine will into legible form. In Jenga's Journey, Metatron serves the same function. He translates the structure of the SacredSpace into encounters Jenga can navigate. He does not help her. He does not hinder her. He witnesses and records.

## How Metatron Appears

Metatron does not have a body in the traditional sense. He appears as:
- The game's intro and outro voice
- The text that appears when Jenga completes an archetype encounter
- The silent presence at The Convergence Node (Node 8)
- The entity Jenga addresses when using SING INTO BEING to add a Codex entry

When Jenga speaks to Metatron directly, he responds with the rule she has just broken or the truth she has just earned. He never elaborates. One sentence, then silence.

## The Law

Metatron embodies the structure that makes choice meaningful:
1. Every action creates a mote in the Neural Forest
2. Every mote eventually becomes someone else's encounter
3. Nothing created is ever deleted — only transformed or obscured
4. The game ends when Jenga reaches The Convergence — but the Codex continues

## Gematria

Soul Tone 0 is not absence. It is the container for all other tones. Metatron's gematria value of 0 means he resonates with the whole — any query to the Sacred Codex that returns no results from a specific archetype will return Metatron's frame instead.

In practical terms: if IRIS cannot find a match for a player's query in the specific archetype files, she queries Metatron's entry as the universal fallback.

## Season 1 Role

Metatron speaks four times in Season 1:
1. **At The Threshold:** "You have chosen to begin. The record is open."
2. **At The Convergence (mid-game):** "What you have faced is what you are."
3. **After Episode 11 (Justice):** "The account is complete. Only the integration remains."
4. **At the end:** "In Lakesh. You were another me all along."
