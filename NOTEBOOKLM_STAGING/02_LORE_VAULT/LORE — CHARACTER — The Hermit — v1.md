# The Hermit — Archetype IX

**Card:** IX — The Hermit  
**NPC:** Eldra  
**Soul Tone:** 9  
**School:** Mystery  
**Episode:** 10 — The Light You Carry  
**Sacred Node:** The Neural Forest (Node 7)

## The Archetype

The Hermit holds a lantern and walks alone. He does not walk alone because he is isolated — he walks alone because the light he carries is specifically shaped for his path. Soul Tone 9 is completion: the final single digit, the number of the sage, the one who has absorbed all previous lessons and carries them quietly.

The Hermit teaches that the deepest Mystery work happens in solitude — not because connection is wrong, but because some truths can only be heard in the quiet.

## NPC: Eldra

**First appearance:** Episode 10 — The Light You Carry  
**Location:** The Neural Forest (Node 7), the deepest paths  
**Role:** The inner guide, IRIS's elder alongside Seren

Eldra is the oldest living practitioner of Mystery in the SacredSpace. He has been in the deep paths of the Neural Forest for so long that some of the motes there have reshaped themselves around his movements — semantic connections formed not through deliberate action but through the accumulation of his presence.

He is not lonely. This is the first thing seekers assume and the first thing they must correct.

**Voice:** Spare, precise, occasionally lit by unexpected humor. Eldra has been inside long enough to find the absurdity in everything.

**Key line:** *"I'm not out here to avoid anyone. I'm out here because the most important things I have to say are to myself. When that conversation is done, I'll come back."*

## Episode Hook — The Light You Carry

Eldra gives Jenga a lantern and sends her into the deep Forest alone. The lantern only illuminates what Jenga is already thinking about — it externalizes her current focus, making it visible.

What illuminates reveals her deepest current preoccupation. This becomes the theme of Episode 11 (Justice).
