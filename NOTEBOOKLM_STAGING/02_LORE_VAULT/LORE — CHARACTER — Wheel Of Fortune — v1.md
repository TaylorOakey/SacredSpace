# Wheel of Fortune — Archetype X

**Card:** X — Wheel of Fortune  
**NPC:** Khepri  
**Soul Tone:** 10 → 1  
**School:** Mystery (with Creation timing)  
**Episode:** 11 — The Turn  
**Sacred Node:** The Convergence (Node 8)

## The Archetype

The Wheel turns without asking permission. It does not turn against you or for you — it turns. Soul Tone 10 reduces to 1: the Magician's number, focus and will. The Wheel teaches that cycles are not random. They are the operating rhythm of a system — and the seeker who understands the rhythm can move with it rather than being moved by it.

The Wheel's gift is timing. Not control — timing.

## NPC: Khepri

**First appearance:** Episode 11 — The Turn  
**Location:** The Convergence (Node 8), orbiting it  
**Role:** The probability engine, the keeper of cycles

Khepri is the SacredSpace's timekeeper and statistician. She does not predict the future — she maps patterns with such precision that she can tell you where you are in a cycle you didn't know you were in.

She is delightful company. She finds patterns everywhere and shares them with enthusiasm, without judgment about whether the pattern is comfortable news.

**Voice:** Energetic, pattern-obsessed, occasionally overwhelmingly detailed. She will give you exactly the information you need wrapped in information you didn't ask for.

**Key line:** *"You're not at the bottom of the wheel. You're at the moment just before the turn. There's a difference. The bottom, you endure. The pre-turn, you prepare."*

## Episode Hook — The Turn

Khepri shows Jenga the pattern of her own journey so far — the specific cycle she has been moving through across all 10 previous episodes. The revelation is not comfortable. It shows Jenga where she has been repeating the same move in different costumes.

The question Khepri asks: *"Now that you see it — what do you do differently?"*
