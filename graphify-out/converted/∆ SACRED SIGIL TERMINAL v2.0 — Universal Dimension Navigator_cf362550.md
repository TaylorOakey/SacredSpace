## THE VISION

Press Cmd+K anywhere. A magic spell appears. Type what you want to do in the sacred universe. The terminal understands. It navigates you through:
- Your memories
- The canon rules
- The Nine Pillars
- The agents and spells
- Your learning progress
- The sacred game world

Works on desktop, phone, browser, embedded in other apps.

1. **∞ VAULT** — Your Obsidian knowledge
2. **◊ GROVE** — Agent routing (IRIS, AURORA, etc.)
3. **∆ FOREST** — Pattern discovery & lore
4. **⊙ CODEX** — Canonical rules
5. **≈ MEMORY** — Your persistent memories
6. **♦ AGENT** — Spell invocation
7. **⊗ SOCIAL** — Community/players

9. **Λ PATH** — Your learning progress
