# The Executive Codex: Dual-Entity Architecture

The structural foundation of SacredSpace relies on a tandem dual-entity framework—a symbiotic but legally distinct relationship between a for-profit creative engine and a tax-exempt stewardship organization. This architecture ensures operational agility, protects intellectual property, and establishes a compliant, sustainable vehicle for philanthropic land acquisition.

## SacredArcana Studios LLC — The Commercial Vanguard
For-profit Limited Liability Company — intellectual property holding company and primary revenue generator
Produces and monetizes sacred creative arts: Jenga’s Journey graphic novel, Seven Sacred Artifacts
Develops zero-cost, open-source technology stack: Python, FastAPI, Ollama, ChromaDB, SQLite
Governed by Chief Cipher under a distinct operating agreement
Concentric four-circle revenue model: free education → print-on-demand → premium memberships → consulting
Systematically funnels designated net profit percentage to the nonprofit’s restricted land acquisition reserve

## SacredSpace Sanctuary 501(c)(3) — The Stewardship Arm
Tax-exempt nonprofit — designed to hold the five-year North Star: the Sacred Little Forest
Governed by an independent Board of Directors under strict non-distribution constraints
Holds physical land in trust, operates community sanctuary, delivers free open-source AI education
Capitalized through public grants, private donations, and structured contributions from the companion LLC
Formal Mission: “Cultivating loving awareness, environmental stewardship, and sovereign technological literacy”

CRITICAL COMPLIANCE RULE: Resource management is strictly unidirectional. The LLC may provide concessions to the nonprofit at below-market rates. Any transactions where the nonprofit pays the LLC must adhere to at-or-below market rates to prevent private benefit violations.
