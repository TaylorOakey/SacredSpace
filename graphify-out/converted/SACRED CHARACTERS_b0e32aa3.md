# 🎲 ARCANA GRID — ALPHA RULESET (PRINT-READY 1-PAGE)
This is written so you can playtest immediately.
## Arcana Grid (Alpha)
Players: 2–4
 Time: 25–45 minutes
 Goal: Achieve 7 Synergy Points OR control 3 Sanctuary Zones at the end of a round.

## Components
1 Grid Board (hand drawn ok)
20–30 Tokens:
Structure (Order tokens)
Energy (Chaos tokens)
Entropy tokens
Synergy markers
2 Mentor Spirit cards:
Mr. Shui Feng
Tempestina Wildroot
10–20 Innovation cards (can be handwritten)

## Setup
Place board center.
Each player chooses a Guardian (or uses Forge card to create one).
Each player starts with:
2 Structure
2 Energy
1 Entropy
Place 3 Sanctuary Zone markers at fixed locations on the grid.

## Turn Structure (Core Loop)
On your turn, do 3 phases:
### 1) Gather (Choose 1)
Gain +1 Structure
 OR
Gain +1 Energy
 OR
Draw 1 Innovation card (if available)
### 2) Act (Choose 1)
Move your Guardian up to 2 spaces
 OR
Place a token (Structure or Energy) on your current tile
 OR
Use Mentor Ability
Order Engine (Shui Feng): move 1 token OR remove 1 Entropy from an adjacent tile
Chaos Engine (Tempestina): add 1 Energy token OR trigger a random event
### 3) Resolve
If Order + Chaos tokens exist on the same tile:
perform Sacred Balance
remove 1 Chaos token → gain +1 Synergy Point
If Entropy is present:
Entropy blocks sanctuary control until cleared

## Sanctuary Zones
A Sanctuary Zone is controlled when a player has:
2 Structure tokens on it
no Entropy on it
Control persists until disrupted.

## Win Conditions (Alpha)
Win immediately if:
You gain 7 Synergy Points, OR
You control 3 Sanctuary Zones at end of round.
