## PART IV — FULFILLMENT RULES

### The Three-Partner System
*(Synthesized from Gemini's partner analysis + ChatGPT's routing logic)*

| Partner | Role | Products | Why |
|---------|------|---------|-----|
| **Gelato** | Primary home décor | Canvas prints, framed art, posters, tapestries | 140+ local partners in 32 countries = fastest delivery + lowest shipping cost for wall art. 70% carbon-neutral shipping. Best for international buyers. |
| **Printful** | Premium quality + apparel | Framed prints, premium tees, embroidered items | In-house production = lowest reshipment rate. White-label packaging for Shopify orders. Quality consistency for collector tier. |
| **Printify** | Volume + variety | Stickers, mugs, totes, blankets, phone cases | Largest product catalog. Best for testing new SKUs. Margins competitive for entry-tier items. |

### Eco-Credibility Rules
*(Synthesized from Gemini's sustainability data: 49% of buyers willing to pay more. ~9–10% premium average per PwC 2024)*

**What we claim only when we can substantiate it:**
- "Locally produced" → only for Gelato orders routed to local partners
- "Organic cotton" → only for Printful GOTS-certified apparel
- "Eco-conscious fulfillment" → accurate globally for Gelato's carbon-neutral shipping

**What we never claim:**
- Vague "sustainable" without specifics
- "Zero carbon" (not accurate for all routes)
- "Ethically made" without knowing specific facility

**Sacred Little Forest product line:** This is the designated eco-first line. All items in this family use Gelato + Printful organic materials where available. Stated in product descriptions.
