## §4 · Intelligence Return Flow

Gemini does not push findings directly into Claude or ChatGPT sessions. Taylor acts as the sovereign bridge.

```
Gemini browses
    → Compiles Grimoire (seven sections)
        → Shares full Grimoire with Taylor
            → Taylor reviews and decides what to route
                → Taylor pastes relevant sections into Claude / ChatGPT session
                    → Council acts on synthesized intel
```

This flow preserves data sovereignty and prevents context contamination across AI instances. Taylor's judgment governs what crosses AI boundaries and when.
