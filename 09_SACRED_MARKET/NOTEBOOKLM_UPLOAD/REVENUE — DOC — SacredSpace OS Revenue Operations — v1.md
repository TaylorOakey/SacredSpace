### **05 / Fulfillment Partners — Who Does What**

**Gelato** (Primary · Home Décor & Wall Art)

* **Best for:** Canvas prints, framed art, posters, tapestries.  
* **Notes:** 140+ local partners, 70% carbon-neutral, fastest international delivery. Direct Etsy/Shopify integration.  
* **Cost:** Free plan / Gelato+ at $14.99/mo.

**Printful** (Premium · Quality \+ Branding)

* **Best for:** Framed prints, premium tees, hoodies.  
* **Notes:** In-house production (lowest reshipment rate), white-label packaging, GOTS organic options.  
* **Cost:** Free / Printful Growth at $24.99/mo.

**Printify** (Volume · SKU Testing & Variety)

* **Best for:** Stickers, mugs, totes, blankets, phone cases.  
* **Notes:** Largest catalog, best entry-tier margins for testing. Multiple print providers per product.  
* **Cost:** Free / Printify Premium at $29/mo.

**Redbubble & TeePublic** (Passive Discovery)

* **Notes:** Zero upfront cost. Lower margins (15–30%) but completely passive with built-in audiences. Upload existing designs as a secondary platform.

**Local Print Lab Note:** For signed limited editions, utilize local archival print labs (or mpix.com). Print 11 units, hand-sign \+ number, package with COA, and ship via USPS Priority Mail.
