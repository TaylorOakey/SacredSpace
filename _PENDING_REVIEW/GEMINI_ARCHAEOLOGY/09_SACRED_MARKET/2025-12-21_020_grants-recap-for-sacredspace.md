## 1. ORGANIZATION OVERVIEW (Grant-Ready)

**Focus Areas:**  
• Arts & cultural expression  
• Environmental stewardship & education  
• Youth & family enrichment  
• Community wellness through creative practice  

**Mission Statement:**  
SacredSpace creates accessible, community-centered programs that reconnect individuals and families to creativity, land, and one another through art, storytelling, and environmental learning.

**Vision:**  
A world where creativity, ecological awareness, and emotional well-being are cultivated together—beginning with families and expanding outward into resilient, grounded communities.

---

## 2. CORE PILOT PROGRAM (THIS IS THE ANCHOR)

### Program Title  
**SacredSpace Pilot: Art, Story, and Nature for Families & Youth**

### Program Summary (Short Version – Use This Often)
The SacredSpace Pilot Program is a community-based initiative that combines visual art, storytelling, environmental education, and family participation into hands-on workshops designed to foster creativity, ecological awareness, and emotional grounding for youth and caregivers.

### Program Description (Expanded)
The SacredSpace Pilot Program offers a series of interactive workshops for children, families, and community members that blend creative arts with environmental learning. Participants engage in storytelling, drawing, symbolic design, plant-based education, and guided creative play that encourages curiosity, emotional expression, and respect for the natural world.

Workshops are designed to be accessible, non-clinical, and inclusive—meeting participants where they are while encouraging imagination, reflection, and shared experience.

---

## 3. COMMUNITY NEED & PROBLEM STATEMENT

Communities increasingly face:
• Limited access to arts education  
• Disconnection from land and nature  
• Rising stress and emotional fatigue among families  
• Few intergenerational creative spaces  

Youth in particular benefit from environments that encourage expression, creativity, and connection beyond screens and standardized structures.

SacredSpace addresses these gaps by offering **low-barrier, community-rooted programs** that support creativity, environmental literacy, and emotional well-being in an integrated, human-centered way.

---

## 4. TARGET POPULATION

• Children (ages ~6–14)  
• Families and caregivers  
• Community members seeking creative, grounding experiences  

Priority is given to:
• Families with limited access to arts programming  
• Youth benefiting from creative and emotional expression  
• Communities seeking low-cost, inclusive enrichment opportunities

## 5. PROGRAM ACTIVITIES (WHAT ACTUALLY HAPPENS)

Each SacredSpace Pilot cycle includes:

• Guided storytelling & creative writing  
• Visual art creation (drawing, symbols, collaborative murals)  
• Nature-based learning (plants, cycles, stewardship)  
• Group reflection and sharing  
• Take-home creative prompts for families  

Activities emphasize participation over performance and curiosity over instruction.
