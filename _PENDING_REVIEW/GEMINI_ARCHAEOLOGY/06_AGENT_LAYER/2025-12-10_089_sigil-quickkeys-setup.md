# ⭐ Option 1: Use **Gboard Custom Text Shortcuts**  
This is the easiest and works system-wide, no apps needed.

### **How it works**
You create a shortcut like:
- `ss` → `S∆CR3DSP@CE`
- `gm` → `G@M∆`
- `src` → `√π∆∆∆S@CRED@SOURCE`

Then whenever you type the shortcut, Gboard automatically offers the sigil-form right above the keyboard.

### **How to Set It Up**
1. Open **Settings** on your phone.  
2. Go to **System**.  
3. Tap **Languages & input**.  
4. Tap **On-screen keyboard** → **Gboard**.  
5. Choose **Dictionary**.  
6. Tap **Personal Dictionary**.  
7. Select your language or **All languages**.  
8. Tap **+**  
   - **Word:** put your sigil version (example: `S∆CR3DSP@CE`)  
   - **Shortcut:** put the letters you want to trigger it (`ss`)  

Repeat for each sacred spelling.
