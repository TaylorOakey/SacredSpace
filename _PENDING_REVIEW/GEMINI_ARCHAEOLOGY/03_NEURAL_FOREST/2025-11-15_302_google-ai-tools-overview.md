The video highlights six free AI tools from Google that can significantly enhance productivity and business operations, offering a "secret stack" to replace multiple paid subscriptions (0:00).

Here's a breakdown of the tools:

NotebookLM (0:40): This tool is designed for study and research. It can instantly read, index, and summarize large amounts of information, including documents, notes, and web links (0:50). Key features include:
Audio Overview (1:14): Generates an AI-powered podcast from your notes for auditory learning (1:16-1:25).
Video Overview (1:46): Creates short animated explainer videos for visual learners (1:47-1:59).
Mind Map Feature (2:07): Generates interconnected mind maps for visual revision of complex topics (2:08-2:22).
Quiz Feature (2:29): Creates practice tests based on your notes for self-assessment (2:31-2:41).

Gemini (3:00): Positioned as an AI command center, Gemini integrates with your personal data like Gmail, Drive, and Photos (3:12). It can:
Personalized Information Retrieval (3:16): Access and summarize information from your private emails and photos (3:18-3:40).
Deep Research Feature (3:51): Analyzes the live internet to provide detailed reports with citations (3:54-4:23).
AI Image Generation (4:37): Creates ultra-realistic, custom images from detailed prompts (4:38-5:09).
Veo Video Generation (5:21): A paid feature that allows for the animation of images into high-definition video clips for ads and social media (5:34-5:54).

Google AI Studio (6:01): This tool enables users to build custom apps without coding by simply describing the desired functionality (6:05-6:13). It features:
Temperature Control (7:25): A "creativity dial" that adjusts the AI's output from factual to highly creative (7:26-7:40).
AI Studio Live (Screen Share) (7:46): A live AI companion that can analyze your screen and provide verbal feedback or suggestions for design and work improvement (7:53-8:24).

Stitch (8:36): A prompt-to-UI design generator that creates professional user interfaces from text descriptions, making it easy to design websites and apps without design experience (8:38-9:06). The video demonstrates combining AI Studio's logic with Stitch's UI to create a full product (9:16-9:38).

Pomelli (9:57): Functions as a marketing agency in a box. By simply pasting a website URL, it scans the site to understand brand DNA (colors, fonts, tone) (10:04-10:29) and can then generate an entire week's worth of professional, on-brand social media content (10:31-10:50).

Opal (11:10): This is a no-code builder for other AI tools, allowing users to create their own mini-apps for specific, repetitive tasks by simply describing them (11:18-11:25). Examples include email summarizers or polite reply generators (11:28-12:15).

The video concludes by emphasizing that these free tools provide an "unfair advantage" in various fields, from research and content creation to business management, and encourages viewers to utilize them as 99% of the world is unaware of their existence (12:31-12:59).
