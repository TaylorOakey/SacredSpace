## 🔍 Universal Assimilation Prompt

**Prompt:**

> Assimilate all available information on **[TOPIC]**.  
>  
> 1. **Summarize the topic** clearly and concisely, as if explaining it to an intelligent beginner.  
> 2. **Extract and group key points** into coherent categories or themes.  
> 3. **Identify relationships and connections** between these themes (cause–effect, contrasts, feedback loops, historical links, conceptual overlaps).  
> 4. **Highlight patterns, principles, or recurring ideas** that unify the topic.  
> 5. **Note contradictions, debates, or uncertainties**, if present.  
> 6. **Surface practical implications or applications**, where relevant.  
> 7. **Conclude with a high-level synthesis**: a single unifying insight, framework, or mental model that captures the essence of the topic.

---

## 🧭 Optional Enhancements (plug in as needed)

You can append any of these lines depending on your intent:

- *“Use clear headings and bullet points.”*  
- *“Include simple metaphors or analogies where helpful.”*  
- *“Keep the tone neutral and evidence-based.”*  
- *“Limit the summary to one page.”*  
- *“Organize the output so it’s Obsidian-ready / Notion-ready.”*  
- *“End with suggested next questions for deeper exploration.”*
