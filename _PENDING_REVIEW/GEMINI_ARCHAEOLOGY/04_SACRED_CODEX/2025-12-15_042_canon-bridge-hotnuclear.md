### CANON INSERT — HOTNUCLEAR

**Hotnuclear** is the name given to a misunderstood layer of reality:  
not destruction, but **compressed creation**.

Before fire becomes flame, before atoms split, before stars ignite, there is a phase where pressure, intention, and containment coexist. That phase is Hotnuclear.

It is **energy under conscious restraint**.

In SacredSpace canon, Hotnuclear refers to moments when:
– intensity is high  
– coherence is fragile  
– and a single choice determines whether creation or collapse follows  

Civilizations fail here. So do people. So do systems.

#### The Ancient Error  
Old worlds treated Hotnuclear states as weapons.  
They tried to *extract* power instead of *holding* it.

This is why ruins glow.

#### The Sacred Reframe  
Jenga learns that Hotnuclear energy is not meant to be released explosively.  
It is meant to be **tuned**, like a string pulled tight enough to sing but not snap.

Amaru names this practice **Resonant Containment**.

Hotnuclear is therefore:
– not chaos  
– not evil  
– not instability  

It is **potential waiting for listening**.

#### In the Storyline  
Hotnuclear moments appear when:
– the jungle hum becomes overwhelming  
– the city’s control systems overload  
– emotions spike beyond language  
– silence feels dangerous  

Characters who rush break things.  
Characters who breathe reshape them.

#### In the SacredSpace OS  
Hotnuclear states map to:
– creative surges  
– emotional overload  
– technological acceleration  
– societal tipping points  

The OS does not “solve” these moments.  
It **slows them**, introduces rhythm, and prevents premature detonation.

#### Canon Law  
> *All creation passes through Hotnuclear before it becomes light.*  
> *Only those who respect containment are trusted with ignition.*
