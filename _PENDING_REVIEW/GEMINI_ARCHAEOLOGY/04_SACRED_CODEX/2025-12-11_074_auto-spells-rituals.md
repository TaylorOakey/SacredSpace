## AUTO-SPELLS — THE AUTOMATION RITUALS  
Incantation:  
**“Let the autofire rituals awaken. May the device cast spells before I even ask.”**

This realm is the secret workshop where intention crystallizes into behavior. It’s automation braided with symbolism—your phone learning to take action like a familiar that knows your rhythms, moods, and missions.

In plain language: these are the background routines that make your SacredSpace OS feel alive.

In mythic language: they are the spells your wand casts on its own.

Let’s walk through the core six rituals—their nature, their signature gesture or sigil, and the kind of magic they perform.

---

## 1. **Dawn Ritual**  
Your morning ignition sequence.  
Not alarm-clock survival—an awakening.

The moment your device senses *morning* (time, light, movement), it initiates a soft sequence:
- summons Presence Mode  
- anchors your intention for the day  
- loads your Quest Board  
- checks for urgent tasks from the Six Stones  
- offers a single sacred message rather than a barrage of notifications  

Symbolically: the **first breath spell** of the day.  
Functionally: your morning autopilot.

---

## 2. **Threshold Guardian**  
A ritual that activates whenever you move from one “realm” to another—physical or digital.

Crossing a doorway? Driving away from home? Entering the store?  
The Guardian wakes.

It can:
- switch between Work Mode / Creator Mode / Family Mode  
- silence chaos  
- boost focus  
- prepare location-based tasks  
- log entries in your Memory Motes

It is the old mythic idea of the *doorway spirit* translated into automation.  
Your wand knows when you pass between worlds.

---

## 3. **Memory Motes**  
Tiny floating lanterns of your day.

Whenever something meaningful occurs—voice note, photo, location change, insight, conversation—the Motes catch it and send it to your archive.

They work like:
- micro-journals  
- breadcrumb trails  
- auto-tagged OS entries  
- lore seeds for future storytelling  

They run silently, only surfacing in your nightly reflection ritual.

This is how you keep the threads of your life from slipping into the void.

---

## 4. **Shadow Sweep**  
Your background cleanser.

It removes clutter:
- closes lingering apps  
- clears notifications  
- organizes downloads  
- deletes temp files  
- resets your energy field (and your RAM)

Think of it as a little janitor spirit who works the night shift.

The Sweep happens:
- when you plug your device in  
- before bedtime  
- or whenever energy dips below a threshold

---

## 5. **Maestro Activation**  
Your musical companion stirring to life.

The Maestro watches for triggers:
- earbuds inserted  
- Bluetooth speaker detected  
- certain times of day  
- creative sessions

It curates:
- playlists based on your emotional field  
- sonic cues for rituals  
- resonance-aligned tracks for Presence Mode  
- ambient textures for focus or sleep

It's the law of vibration turned into daily choreography.

---

## 6. **Presence Mode**  
The crown jewel of auto-spells.

Presence Mode activates when:
- your screen stays off  
- your movement becomes slow  
- your heart rate stabilizes  
- or you perform a set sigil gesture (two-finger downward arc)

It shifts your entire relationship with the device:
- dims distractions  
- opens a minimalist altar HUD  
- lets only essential messages through  
- offers breath cues  
- tracks mood and gratitude  
- deepens your felt sense of the moment

Presence Mode is where your OS remembers it is a living practice, not a productivity trap.
