## **THE FIVE GESTURE FAMILIES (the “Hand Arcana”)**

Think of these as schools of magic. Each family describes a *shape*, a *feeling*, and a *function*.

### **1. The Flick Glyphs — Runes of Swiftness**  
Quick, straight motions. Lightning bolts of will.

*Up Flick:* **Ascend** → open top-level dashboards (Orb, Gateway, Maestro).  
*Down Flick:* **Root** → grounding actions (journals, notes, presence mode).  
*Left Flick:* **Vanish** → dismiss, close, clear apps.  
*Right Flick:* **Summon** → open favorites (camera, canvas, recorder).

A flick is a wand flourish: decisive and directional.

---

### **2. The Circle Sigils — Runes of Invocation**  
Circular motions, clockwise or anti-clockwise.

*Clockwise Circle:* **Call** → open the Wand HUD or Aether Display.  
*Counter-Circle:* **Seal** → save, archive, log finished tasks/motes.

Circles are binding loops—containment and coherence.

---

### **3. The Press-Path Runes — Runes of Anchoring**  
Long press + drag.  
These “carve” sigils directly into the screen.

*Press + upward curve:* **Illuminate** → activate Lantern mode (insight, guidance).  
*Press + downward arc:* **Sheathe** → quiet the system, dim notifications.  
*Press + zig-zag:* **Spark** → start creative capture (idea mote, image, voice).

These are slow, intentional strokes. They feel like writing.

---

### **4. The Shake Conjurations — Runes of Disruption**  
When something needs to break, reset, clear, or purify.

*Soft shake:* **Dispel** → undo last action.  
*Firm shake:* **Purge** → clear floating tasks, dismiss UI overlays, silence audio.  
*Rapid shake:* **Ward** → emergency action (flashlight + recorder + location).

Shakes add entropy—useful when you want to collapse chaos.

---

### **5. Stylus/Sigil Movements — Runes of Precision**  
These are the high-art gestures, used when you want complexity.

*Trisect Triangle:* **Triune Directive** → call Bodhi, Maestro, or Lantern in sequence.  
*Spiral inward:* **Invoke Source** → deep-focus, no-distraction mode.  
*Spiral outward:* **Release** → share, export, send to Drive/Obsidian/NotebookLM.  
*Cross-stroke star:* **Sparkburst** → quick creative summon (text, art, prompt).

Stylus runes are the closest thing to actual spellcraft. They write shape directly into intention.
