### 🌿 **1. TYPES OF ART EVENTS FOR SACRED SPACES**
These events should reflect your mission of grounding, cultivation, and connection:

#### 🎨 Interactive Art
- **Live Mural Painting**
- **Community Mandala Creation** (chalk, flower petals, etc.)
- **Sacred Symbols Workshop** (tribal patterns, spiritual geometry)

#### 🌱 Nature-Based Art
- **Natural Dye & Fabric Painting**
- **Earth Altar Installations**
- **Clay & Mud Sculpture Sessions**

#### 🕊️ Performance Art
- **Spoken Word / Poetry Circles**
- **Shamanic Dance or Ecstatic Movement**
- **Drum & Fire Circles with Visual Projections**

#### 🌀 Healing + Creation
- **Art Therapy Corners** (open canvas, journaling, watercolor)
- **Intuitive Painting & Sound Journeys**
- **Cacao & Creativity Ceremonies**

### 📍**2. WHERE TO HOST ART EVENTS**
Opportunities can range from local partnerships to larger cultural events:

#### 🏕️ Nature & Retreat Spaces
- Campgrounds
- State parks with group shelters
- Permaculture farms
- Private landowners open to conscious events

#### 🏛️ Local Venues
- Art galleries or museums with spiritual themes
- Community centers, libraries
- Cafes/shops with open walls
- Yoga studios or holistic healing centers

#### 🎪 Pop-Up + Market Events
- Farmer’s Markets
- Spiritual Festivals (like Sacred Earth, SoulQuest, etc.)
- Art walks & cultural nights in nearby towns

---

### 🔍 **3. HOW TO FIND OPPORTUNITIES**
Use this as a checklist:

#### 🔸 Local Event Boards / Calendars
- Facebook Events
- Eventbrite
- Meetup.com (search "healing", "art", "yoga", "open mic", etc.)
- Local tourism & arts councils

#### 🔸 Direct Partnerships
- Reach out to small businesses with aligned values
- Visit local holistic shops and see if they’d co-host
- Talk with permaculture farmers or retreat organizers

#### 🔸 Build Your Own:
- Launch a **monthly Sacred Spaces Circle** (start small)
- Use your **graphic novel + brand** to attract interest
- Start with **low-cost or donation-based events** to build traction
