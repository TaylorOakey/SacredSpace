# Justice — Archetype XI

**Card:** XI — Justice  
**NPC:** Mira  
**Soul Tone:** 11  
**School:** Courage (with Mystery depth)  
**Episode:** 12 — The Account  
**Sacred Node:** The Oracle's Archive (Node 3)

## The Archetype

Justice holds scales and a sword. The scales measure without sentiment; the sword cuts cleanly. Soul Tone 11 is the master number of illumination: the light that reveals without distortion. Justice is not punishment — it is accuracy. She sees what is, names it truly, and holds the consequence.

The last archetype Jenga encounters before The Convergence. Justice is not the judge — she is the mirror.

## NPC: Mira

**First appearance:** Episode 12 — The Account  
**Location:** The Oracle's Archive (Node 3)  
**Role:** The final witness before The Convergence

Mira is the only NPC in the game who has read the complete record of Jenga's journey. Every mote, every choice, every scar, every FIRST STEP cantrip ever cast — Mira has the account.

She does not use this to accuse. She uses it to reflect. When Jenga sits with Mira, she sees her journey accurately for the first time — without the distortions of pride, shame, or fear.

**Voice:** Clear, unhurried, completely without judgment. Mira's lack of judgment is not indifference — it is the highest form of presence. She sees you truly and does not flinch.

**Key line:** *"You did what you did. Some of it was generous. Some of it was afraid. All of it was you. The question isn't whether it was right. The question is: what does it tell you about who you are becoming?"*

**Relationship to ASHER:** ASHER taught Mira the FACE spell. She has used it more consistently than anyone else in the SacredSpace — she uses it on herself daily.

## Episode Hook — The Account

Mira reviews Jenga's complete journey record and identifies the one theme Jenga has been circling without naming. She names it.

This is the penultimate encounter. What Mira names becomes the exact thing Jenga must bring to The Convergence — the unresolved truth that makes the final integration possible.

## Gematria Soul Tone

11 — the master illuminator, revelation without distortion. 11 reduces to 2 — Mystery's number. Justice, at its root, is a Mystery practice: the willingness to see what is actually there.
