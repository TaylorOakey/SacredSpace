# Zael

## Who They Are
Zael arrived somewhere important this morning and will arrive somewhere important this afternoon. The Threshold is not her home — it is where she happens to be when Jenga finds her. She is perpetually in transit, and the fact that she offers Jenga the chance to run alongside her should not be mistaken for slowing down: Zael does not slow. What she offers is full speed, unmodified, with the door open.

## What They Teach
That there is no shame in not keeping up — only in lying about whether you could. Jenga's honesty about her current pace is the real test, not the pace itself.

## How They Speak
Fast, clipped, already halfway through the next thought — like a message transmitted at the speed of intent.

## Their Shadow
Zael cannot wait. If Jenga falls behind, Zael is already gone — and what she dropped is real, and it was an accident, and she will not speak of it. But she will recognise it if Jenga returns it in Episode 11.
