# Seren

## Who They Are
Seren inhabits the space between the question and the answer — which is to say, Seren inhabits most of the Oracle's Archive. They are the keeper of questions never asked, and those questions carry weight in their keeping. Seren does not withhold; they wait for the asker to be ready. They have been waiting for some of those questions longer than the Archive has had shelves.

## What They Teach
That the question you avoid is the one doing the most work on you. The answer Jenga chooses not to receive becomes the lens through which all later episodes are filtered.

## How They Speak
Slow, with the quality of still water — reflective, unhurried, impossible to rush.

## Their Shadow
Seren holds the question Jenga didn't choose. In Episode 12, that question returns — through Mira's mouth, at the moment of account. Seren never forgets what was declined.
