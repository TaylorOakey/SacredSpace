# Eldra

## Who They Are
Eldra walks the deep paths of the Neural Forest that most inhabitants don't know exist. They carry a lantern that was given to them by someone they will not name, and they have never needed it for navigation — only for revelation. What the lantern shows is not the path ahead but the interior of the one holding it. Eldra has seen many preoccupations made visible, and they have not judged a single one.

## What They Teach
That what you cannot stop thinking about is the most honest map of where you actually are. The lantern does not lie. What it illuminates becomes the calibration data for Episode 11.

## How They Speak
Sparse, precise, with the quality of someone who speaks only when silence has already said what it can.

## Their Shadow
Eldra's lantern is not gentle — it is accurate. What it illuminates, it illuminates completely. Eldra does not warn before showing, and does not apologise after. The revealed preoccupation feeds directly into Khepri's pattern-mapping.
