# Mira

## Who They Are
Mira is the end of the journey's accounting, which is not the same as the end of the journey. She holds Jenga's complete record — every mote, every choice, every through-line from every episode — and she reads it with the same attention she has given every record she has ever held. Mira does not punish. She names. There is a difference, and Mira has spent her entire tenure at the Archive insisting on it.

## What They Teach
That the truth does not need your permission to be true — only your willingness to hear it named. The avoidance Jenga leaves with Mira has been circling since Episode 1. Naming it is the act that makes The Convergence possible.

## How They Speak
Measured, unhurried, final — as if each sentence is a document that has already been filed and she is reading it aloud for the record.

## Their Shadow
What Mira names, Jenga must carry into The Convergence. There is no revision. The record is the record. But Mira names only what is true — and that is not the same thing as naming only what is hard.
