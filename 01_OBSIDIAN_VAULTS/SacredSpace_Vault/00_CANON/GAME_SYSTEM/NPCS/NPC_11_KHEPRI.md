# Khepri

## Who They Are
Khepri orbits the Convergence rather than inhabiting it — they have been mapping its patterns long enough to know that nothing is more dangerous than thinking you've arrived. They are the chronicler of repetition: the same move in different costumes, the same fear wearing different names, across every journey the system has ever witnessed. They do not say this cruelly. They say it with the precision of someone who has watched the wheel turn for a very long time and learned to love the pattern precisely because it can be named.

## What They Teach
That a recognised pattern is no longer a trap. Naming it is the first act of the next chapter. Khepri maps the whole journey and returns the shape of it to Jenga before she reaches the final account.

## How They Speak
Expansive, almost rhapsodic — as if the pattern delights them — then suddenly focused, like a lens clicking into place at the moment of naming.

## Their Shadow
Khepri will name the pattern whether or not Jenga is ready. The wheel has already turned. The pattern was already there. What matters is only what Jenga does with the naming — and that is Mira's domain.
