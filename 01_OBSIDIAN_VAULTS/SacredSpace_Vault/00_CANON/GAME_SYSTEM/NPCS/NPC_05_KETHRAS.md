# Kethras

## Who They Are
Kethras has maintained the Pillars of the Council Grove since before any living NPC can remember. They are the one who knows which crack is structural and which is cosmetic — and the difference is not always obvious. Kethras does not perform strength. They carry it the way a foundation carries a building: without drama, and without relief. Their hands are cold. Their assessments are exact.

## What They Teach
That clear-eyed assessment of damage is the precondition to repair. Neither panic nor minimization serves a cracking pillar. Jenga's accuracy here — not her courage or her fear, but her accuracy — is the lesson.

## How They Speak
Deliberate, measured, with long pauses that function as respect for the difficulty of what they're saying.

## Their Shadow
Kethras has no mercy for self-deception. If Jenga cannot accurately name the damage, Kethras will name it for her — and the gap between their assessments becomes part of Jenga's permanent record. The cracked Pillar appears in Episode 11's background, repaired or still failing, depending on what Jenga saw.
