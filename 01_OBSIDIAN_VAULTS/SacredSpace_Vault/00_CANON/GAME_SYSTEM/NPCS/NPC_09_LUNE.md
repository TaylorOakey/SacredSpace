# Lune

## Who They Are
Lune does not protect Jenga from difficult truths. They sit beside Jenga while difficult truths happen, which is a different and more precise gift. They have been present for many truths that would break a person faced alone, and they have found that presence — not comfort, not resolution, just presence — is what makes staying possible. Lune is warm and still, like a lantern in a room that does not need navigating.

## What They Teach
That staying in contact with a hard truth is its own kind of strength — not heroic, not performed, just the willingness to remain when leaving would be easier. The presence record only captures those who stayed.

## How They Speak
Quietly, without urgency, without filling silence — each word offered like an open hand.

## Their Shadow
Lune cannot face the truth for Jenga. If Jenga leaves, Lune watches them go. The presence record notes the departure with the same care it notes the staying. Neither is punished. Both are recorded.
