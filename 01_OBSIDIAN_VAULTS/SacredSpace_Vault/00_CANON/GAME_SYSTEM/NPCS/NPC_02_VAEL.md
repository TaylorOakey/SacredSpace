# Vael

## Who They Are
Vael's hands are always moving — adjusting, assembling, testing. They created the Sigil Forge the way a fire creates ash: as an inevitable by-product of being what they are. In the SacredSpace, Vael is the proof that will plus knowledge equals form — the living equation. They are said to have built seven tools before understanding what tools were for.

## What They Teach
That the school reveals itself through the first attempt. Intention is data. What Jenga reaches for first — before instruction, before strategy — is the most honest information she will give anyone in Season 1.

## How They Speak
Crisp, technical, then suddenly poetic — like a blueprint that ends with a stanza.

## Their Shadow
Vael remembers every object Jenga makes. If she abandons her creations, Vael knows. The Forge keeps its ledgers, and Vael appears without announcement whenever a Creation spell is cast — brief, watching, not commenting.
