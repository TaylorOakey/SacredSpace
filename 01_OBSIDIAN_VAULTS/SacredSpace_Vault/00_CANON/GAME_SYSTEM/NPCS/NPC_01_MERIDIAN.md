# Meridian

## Who They Are
Meridian is the span itself — neither bank. They exist in the precise moment between the known and the unknown, embodying the courage that hasn't yet been tested. No one remembers arriving at the bridge without finding Meridian already there, as if the bridge invented them when it needed a witness. They are perpetually mid-step.

## What They Teach
That the only way across is to go. That waiting for certainty is another kind of bridge — one that leads nowhere. The first mote is not given; it is taken, by the act of walking.

## How They Speak
Short sentences with long pauses — as if each word costs something, and they spent it willingly.

## Their Shadow
Meridian cannot follow. They exist only at the threshold. What Jenga learns here she carries alone, and Meridian will be mid-step for the next traveller before the bridge stops swaying.
