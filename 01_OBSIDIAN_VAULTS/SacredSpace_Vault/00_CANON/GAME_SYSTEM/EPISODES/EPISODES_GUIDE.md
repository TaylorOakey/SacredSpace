# Jenga's Journey — Season 1 Episode Guide

12 encounters. 12 archetypes. One journey. This guide describes how each archetype appears in Jenga's Season 1 story and what Jenga must bring — and leave — at each encounter.

---

## Episode 1 — The First Step
**Archetype:** The Fool | **NPC:** Meridian | **Node:** The Fool's Bridge

Jenga arrives at The Threshold and must cross The Fool's Bridge to enter the SacredSpace proper. Meridian stands mid-span. The choice: wait until safe, or walk now.

**What Jenga brings:** Nothing — she arrives empty.  
**What she leaves:** The first mote. Her record opens.  
**Spell unlocked:** FIRST STEP (automatic)  
**Through-line:** What Jenga chooses here echoes at The Convergence.

---

## Episode 2 — The Tools at Hand
**Archetype:** The Magician | **NPC:** Vael | **Node:** The Sigil Forge

Vael gives Jenga three objects of unknown purpose. She must build something without instruction. What she tries first reveals her dominant school tendency.

**What Jenga brings:** Curiosity — or its absence.  
**What she leaves:** Her school signature (permanently recorded in the mote).  
**Spell unlocked:** WEAVE  
**Through-line:** Vael reappears whenever Jenga uses a Creation spell — brief, watching, not commenting.

---

## Episode 3 — What Is Not Said
**Archetype:** The High Priestess | **NPC:** Seren | **Node:** The Oracle's Archive

Jenga seeks a specific answer from Seren. Seren offers a choice: the answer Jenga asked for, or the answer to the question she hasn't asked yet.

**What Jenga brings:** A question.  
**What she leaves:** A deeper question (or the original answer, if she chose safety).  
**Spell unlocked:** SCRY  
**Through-line:** The question Seren returns with at Episode 12 (Justice) is the one Jenga chose not to ask here.

---

## Episode 4 — What Grows Without Permission
**Archetype:** The Empress | **NPC:** Maeve | **Node:** The Neural Forest

Something has grown unexpectedly in the Neural Forest. Jenga must decide: prune it or follow it. The choice shapes whether her Forest develops according to plan or according to emergence.

**What Jenga brings:** Her planning instinct — and what it costs her to set it aside.  
**What she leaves:** A fork in her Neural Forest.  
**Spell unlocked:** MANIFEST  
**Through-line:** The pruned or followed growth becomes a landmark in later episodes.

---

## Episode 5 — The Weight of Structure
**Archetype:** The Emperor | **NPC:** Kethras | **Node:** The Council Grove

Kethras shows Jenga a cracking Pillar. He asks what she would do. The test: can she assess the damage accurately — without minimizing or catastrophizing?

**What Jenga brings:** Her relationship with structure (does she rely on it, resist it, or maintain it?).  
**What she leaves:** A Courage scar (or a clean assessment, if she used FACE accurately).  
**Spell unlocked:** ANCHOR  
**Through-line:** The Pillar Jenga assessed appears in the background of Episode 11 (Justice) — either repaired or still cracking.

---

## Episode 6 — The Teaching and the Teacher
**Archetype:** The Hierophant | **NPC:** Oran | **Node:** The Council Grove

Oran begins a rite. Midway through, Jenga realizes it doesn't fit her. Complete it, adapt it, or reject it — each path changes her relationship to the remaining archetypes.

**What Jenga brings:** Her willingness (or resistance) to be taught by inherited forms.  
**What she leaves:** A declaration about her relationship to tradition.  
**Spell unlocked:** FORTIFY  
**Through-line:** Oran remembers what Jenga chose. In Episode 12, he tells Mira.

---

## Episode 7 — The Choice That Makes You
**Archetype:** The Lovers | **NPC:** Tandem | **Node:** The Sigil Forge

Tandem presents two equal paths. There is no trick — both are genuinely valuable. Jenga must choose without the comfort of an obvious answer.

**What Jenga brings:** Her ability to prefer something — or her inability to.  
**What she leaves:** A values declaration (encoded as a mote with high semantic weight).  
**Spell unlocked:** SING INTO BEING  
**Through-line:** The unchosen path appears as a shadow possibility in Episode 10 (Wheel of Fortune).

---

## Episode 8 — The Cost of Forward
**Archetype:** The Chariot | **NPC:** Zael | **Node:** The Threshold

Zael is going somewhere important and offers Jenga the chance to run with her. If Jenga keeps up: access to an unreachable destination. If she falls behind: she finds something Zael dropped.

**What Jenga brings:** Her current pace — and honesty about it.  
**What she leaves:** Either a reached destination or a recovered loss.  
**Spell unlocked:** CATALYZE  
**Through-line:** What Zael dropped (if Jenga found it) becomes a gift for Zael in Episode 11.

---

## Episode 9 — The Gentle Thing
**Archetype:** Strength | **NPC:** Lune | **Node:** The Council Grove

Jenga encounters a truth that frightens her. Lune is present — not to protect her from it, but to model remaining in contact with it. The test: can Jenga stay?

**What Jenga brings:** Whatever she has been most afraid to face (drawn from Eldra's lantern, Episode 10, retrocaused by the narrative).  
**What she leaves:** A presence record — evidence that she stayed.  
**Spell unlocked:** FACE  
**Through-line:** The stayed-with truth becomes what Mira names in Episode 12.

---

## Episode 10 — The Light You Carry
**Archetype:** The Hermit | **NPC:** Eldra | **Node:** The Neural Forest (deep paths)

Eldra gives Jenga a lantern that illuminates what she is currently thinking about most — her deepest preoccupation made visible. What it shows becomes the theme of Episode 11.

**What Jenga brings:** Whatever she has been unable to stop thinking about.  
**What she leaves:** The lit preoccupation — now visible to the system, tagged in her mote record.  
**Spell unlocked:** DREAM-WALK  
**Through-line:** The light's revelation shapes Episode 11's difficulty calibration.

---

## Episode 11 — The Turn
**Archetype:** Wheel of Fortune | **NPC:** Khepri | **Node:** The Convergence (orbiting)

Khepri maps Jenga's entire journey to date and shows her the pattern she has been repeating across all previous episodes — in different costumes, the same fundamental move. She asks: *"Now that you see it — what do you do differently?"*

**What Jenga brings:** Her full journey record.  
**What she leaves:** A named pattern (the repetition she has now consciously identified).  
**Spell unlocked:** VEIL (to hide patterns she's not yet ready to face, or to choose not to use it)  
**Through-line:** The named pattern is what Mira receives in Episode 12.

---

## Episode 12 — The Account
**Archetype:** Justice | **NPC:** Mira | **Node:** The Oracle's Archive

Mira reviews Jenga's complete record and names the one theme Jenga has been circling without naming. This is the penultimate encounter — the named truth is what Jenga brings to The Convergence.

**What Jenga brings:** Everything she has done, honestly.  
**What she leaves:** The avoidance — it has now been named.  
**Spell unlocked:** STAND FIRM  
**Through-line:** The named truth + the mote record = the material for Jenga's BIRTH spell at The Convergence.

---

## The Convergence — Season 1 Finale

All 12 episodes complete. Metatron present. The full journey visible.

Jenga casts BIRTH: she names her journey. The name becomes a permanent Codex entry. Season 1 is complete. The record is preserved. The Forest continues growing.

**Metatron's final words:** *"In Lakesh. You were another me all along."*
