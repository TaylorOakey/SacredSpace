# Sacred Node 8 — The Convergence

**Type:** Integration and Completion Space  
**Guardian:** Metatron  
**Unlock Condition:** Complete all 12 archetype encounters.  
**Associated Archetype:** Metatron (The Law) — all archetypes converge here  
**Element:** All four, resolved into one

## Location Description

The Convergence is where all paths in the SacredSpace meet. It does not appear on the map at the start of Jenga's Journey — it cannot be found, only arrived at. After the final encounter (Episode 12 / Justice / Mira), the SacredSpace reconfigures itself and The Convergence becomes accessible.

It appears differently for every seeker. What is consistent: it is the size of a thought. It contains everything Jenga has done. It is quiet.

Metatron is present — not as a figure but as an orientation. The sense that everything here has been witnessed and recorded and is now complete.

## What Happens Here

This is not a test. The Convergence is not a final boss. It is an integration space — the place where Jenga's full journey is laid out, and she is invited to see it whole for the first time.

**Metatron speaks here for the second time this visit:** *"What you have faced is what you are."* (He said this once before, mid-game. It means something different now.)

**The complete mote record** of Jenga's journey is visible here — every FIRST STEP cantrip, every FACE spell, every scar from failed Courage casts, every SING INTO BEING Codex entry. The full Neural Forest of her specific journey.

**Integration mechanic:** Jenga can name her journey. The name becomes a permanent Codex entry. This is her BIRTH spell — the completion of the full creative cycle begun when she first stepped onto The Fool's Bridge.

## Game Mechanics

**After The Convergence:**
- Jenga's mote record is preserved in the Neural Forest permanently
- The Codex entry she created at The Convergence is added to the Archive
- A new Jenga — a new seeker — can begin a new journey, and will encounter the traces of the previous one in the Forest

**Metatron's final line:** *"In Lakesh. You were another me all along."*

## Lore Note

The Convergence is proof that the game is not circular — it is spiral. Jenga ends where she began (at The Threshold of a new understanding) but one full turn higher. The journey does not repeat. It continues, transformed.

Season 2 begins here.
