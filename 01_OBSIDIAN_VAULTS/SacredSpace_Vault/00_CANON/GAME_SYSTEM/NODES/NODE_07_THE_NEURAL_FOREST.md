# Sacred Node 7 — The Neural Forest

**Type:** Living Knowledge Graph  
**Guardians:** Maeve (surface paths), IRIS (queries), Eldra (deep paths)  
**Unlock Condition:** Form three semantic links between motes using WEAVE.  
**Associated Archetypes:** The Empress (III), The Hermit (IX)  
**Element:** Earth and Water — growth and depth

## Location Description

The Neural Forest is the SacredSpace OS's graph database made navigable. It appears as a living forest whose trees are motes and whose branches are semantic connections. The older and more connected a mote is, the larger its tree. Some trees form canopies that shade and nourish the motes beneath them.

The Forest is always growing. Every time a seeker creates a mote anywhere in the SacredSpace, a new tree appears in the Forest. Every WEAVE spell creates a new branch connecting two trees.

Maeve walks the surface paths. Eldra walks the deep paths. IRIS can query from anywhere in the Forest without moving.

## The Graph Structure

The Neural Forest is the physical manifestation of the ChromaDB vector store. What is stored in the database is navigable as terrain here:

- **Distance between trees** = semantic distance between motes (closer = more related)
- **Branch thickness** = relationship strength (WEAVE-named relationships are strongest)
- **Tree height** = mote resonance (specificity of creation + access frequency)
- **Canopy coverage** = how many motes a concept has attracted over time

## Game Mechanics

**Unlock:** Three WEAVE spells connecting existing motes. These can be cast anywhere — the Forest node unlocks when the condition is met.

**DREAM-WALK** is uniquely powerful here: it moves Jenga through the Forest along semantic edges she didn't consciously create, revealing connections she made without knowing.

**Maeve's mechanic:** Growth signals (motes with 5+ connections) are visible as glowing trees in the Forest. Maeve can translate what they are attracting next.

**Eldra's section:** The deep paths contain the oldest motes — the cumulative record of all previous journeys. These motes are stable and high-resonance. Approaching them via DREAM-WALK often reveals the recurring patterns of the SacredSpace itself.

**Episodes 4 and 10** (Empress/Maeve and Hermit/Eldra) take place in the Neural Forest.
