## ACT IV — THE FIVE BLIND SPOTS

The comprehensive session report (Sessions 22–25) was passed to Claude.ai for independent strategic analysis. Claude affirmed the work as "the most structurally significant session" but identified five critical blind spots:

### Blind Spot 1 — The 84-File Debt (🔴)
84 of 89 extracted conversations remain un-reviewed. These contain the origin story, memory architecture, agent designs, and economic models missing from local code. The longer they sit, the more local implementation drifts from the Gemini-era vision.

### Blind Spot 2 — Origin Story Criticality (🔴)
The SacredSpace origin story — the soul contract, founding intent — exists only in Gemini-era conversations. Without a canonized origin, future decisions lack anchor. Every feature and pivot must be measured against "why SacredSpace exists."

### Blind Spot 3 — Bodhilyra Orb Canon Risk (🔴)
The Orb is the central architectural concept of the Gemini era — a listening center, intent router, relevance decider. It has no local equivalent. Every system built without it may need fundamental restructuring when it arrives.

### Blind Spot 4 — GPT Config Sleeper Priority (🟡)
Eight ChatGPT GPT system prompts exist in Gemini conversations — months of prompt engineering. Cataloged but not ported to local configs. Porting them reclaims months of prior investment.

### Blind Spot 5 — LΨR∆ Gate Still Open (🔴)
The LΨR∆ (LYRA) gate — a core architectural threshold — was identified as open in earlier sessions and never resolved. Open gates are structural vulnerabilities.

### The Priority Restack

Claude's recommended build order — foundation before feature:

```
1. 🔴 01_CORE origin story canonization

4. 🔴 LΨR∆ gate resolution
```
